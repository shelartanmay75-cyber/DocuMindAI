How It Works

True RAG — your content is extracted in the browser, then sent to Claude AI

01
📤

Upload & Extract

Your file is parsed right in the browser. PDF.js reads every page of a PDF, Mammoth parses Word, SheetJS reads Excel — all text extracted locally.

02
🔍

Content Is Sent to Claude

The extracted text becomes the context window. Claude reads your actual document — no guessing, no hallucinations about what might be in it.

03
💬

Grounded Answers

Every answer is derived from your document's real content. If it's not in the file, Claude says so — complete honesty about what the document contains.
